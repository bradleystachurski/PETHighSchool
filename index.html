Welcome to the PET - High School Project

PET-HS is an innovatve, student-centered physics curriculum that is designed to engage students in scientific reasoning and follows a guided scientfic model- building approach. PET-HS is based on five design principles drawn from sociocultural and cognitve research that allow classrooms to become inclusive learning environments where students develop, share, cri8que, argue and revise evidence- based ideas.
